MY RECOMMENDATIONS:


If you are looking for a light-duty, comfortable truck,
primarily for driving around but with some punch for real work,
I would recommend the Chevrolet 1500 with either V8 options (5.3L or 6.2L).

  1. chevrolet 1500 6.2L v8
    • 420 hp
    • 475 lb-ft torque
    • ~15-16 mpg city / 20 mpg highway


  2. chevrolet 1500 5.3L v8
    • 355 hp
    • 383 lb-ft torque
    • 16-17 MPG city and 20-21 MPG highway





If you need some real power to the tires for hauling and towing, you cannot beat the F-series trucks.
The F-350 or F-450 have incredible stats, and testimonials to back them.
Powerstroke diesel engines are true workhorses, and in my opinion beat out the Duramax counterparts.
  1. ford f-450
    • 6.7L Power Stroke V8 Turbo Diesel
    • 475 hp/1,050 lb-ft torque
    • Max Towing Capacity: ~40,000 lbs


  2. chevrolet Silverado HD 3500
    • 6.6L Duramax V8 Turbo-Diesel
    • 470 hp/975 lb-ft torque
    • Max Towing Capacity: ~36,000 lbs
